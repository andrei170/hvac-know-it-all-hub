The four installs sequenced

Install order Piece Why this order
1 The scorecard quiz + email capture Without the list, every other piece below leaks the audience. The scorecard ships first to build the asset that compounds.
2 The 5-7 min homepage VSL + the Business Track landing page The landing is the destination the scorecard, the bio links, and the in-app tile route into. The VSL is the pre-sell that lifts the landing's cold conversion.
3 The segmented welcome flows (free-app / scorecard / paid-buyer) The flows turn the captured contacts into a nurture surface. Without them, the list sits idle.
4 The in-app Business Track discovery tile The app surface is additive - it amplifies the discovery once the scorecard, landing, and flows are live. Wired last because it depends on the destinations above.

Install timeline (less than 14 days from kickoff)

Day Work
Day 1 Brand colour lock + voice calibration session. Scorecard questions drafted. Track A / Track B curriculum outline locked (Gary writes the curriculum, AG1 maps it to the landing). Welcome-flow tier map. Application Typeform field map.
Day 2 VSL script v1 in Gary's voice (operator-grounded). Scorecard logic + routing wired. Business Track landing wireframed. Email platform integration scaffolded. Webhook scaffolded for the in-app tile.
Day 3 VSL film day (Gary on camera, AG1 directs). Scorecard live on staging. Business Track landing v1 copy. Welcome-flow sequences drafted (3 sequences).
Day 4 VSL edit + captions. Scorecard QA across mobile + desktop. Business Track landing build. Welcome-flow sequences loaded into the email platform.
Day 5 VSL embed live on hvacknowitall.com hero + Business Track landing hero. Scorecard public. Application Typeform live. Tracking pixel + GA4 fired.
Day 6 Email nurture (scorecard fork + post-application) loaded + tested. Segmented welcome flow tested end-to-end (free-app install + scorecard completion + paid-buyer purchase).
Day 7 Linktree + YouTube end-screen cards + podcast show notes + Facebook group pinned post + Instagram Stories highlight updated. Pinned-comment template loaded for Gary's video-upload workflow.
Day 8 In-app discovery tile coordinated with Gary's app developer. Deeplink + tracking pixel wired.
Day 9 Full funnel walkthrough end-to-end: cold YouTube viewer routes through scorecard, into Track A landing, into application, into approval, into welcome flow. Walk every fork.
Day 10 First cohort cycle dates locked. Kickoff campaign drafted (single email blast + Instagram Stories sequence + podcast pre-roll + Facebook group post).
Day 11-14 Buffer for any blocker, Gary copy sign-off line-by-line, final QA across mobile + desktop, kickoff campaign ship date locked.

Slower-lever items (Day-60 plus, not on the install clock)

  • Paid Meta + Google ads to the scorecard
  • Alumni community launch (post-first-cohort-graduation)
  • Patreon-style monthly recurring tier underneath the cohort
  • Paid retargeting on scorecard-abandoners
  • Second product surface beyond the two-track Business Track (e.g. quarterly in-person operator summit)
  • White-label Business Track licensing to provincial trade-college partnerships
  • Sponsor-segment upgrade pitch using the new segmented email list

These are real revenue levers and they belong on the post-Day-14 roadmap. They are not on the Day-1-to-Day-14 install clock.

The split: Gary owns the curriculum and the delivery. AG1 owns the funnel infrastructure underneath. The handoff line is the application approval - up to that point the funnel is AG1-built, from that point forward the cohort is Gary-delivered.

Compliance footer

The Business Track is operator development for the shop-owner-track technician. Not a substitute for ticketed trade training (TSSA / EPA Section 608 / provincial trade colleges / manufacturer-certified programs). Results vary by market, capital, and operator execution.
